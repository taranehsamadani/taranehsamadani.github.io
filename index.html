The Lyrebirds Podcast: Don't F*ck With Cats

It is no secret that social media has had an intense effect on the ways that people view the world around them. In terms of serious matters, including crime coverage and the opinions surrounding it, there is absolutely no exception. A discussion between students, who are very aware of the media and how it works and how the use of social media has had an effect on well-known crimes of the past, showcases just how this trend can continue to affect the motions and outcomes of crimes of the future. In this podcast, The Lyrebids Pocast: Don't F*ck With Cats, we discuss the popular Netflix documentary and how it relates to the social media as a whole.

Research Paper

My freshman year of college I had to write a research paper on something I was passionate about that related to the media. I chose to write about the dangers or media and technology for children. I think that many of the new social medias apps, like Tik Tok, are very damaging for the youth. The article is aimed toward Keeping Kids Safe on Technology and the Media. I know a lot of people may not agree with my position on this topic, but I think it is important for parents to really think about what they are allowing their children to view and monitor how much screen time they are getting.
